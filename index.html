About Me

Hello! I’m Vishnu, a software developer living in New York. I’ve been programming for a little over two years since graduating from the TechHire OpenCode bootcamp. I have previously tutored students in software development and have some experience working in a startup environment. I’ve always had an interest in creating things ever since I was a kid. Whenever the power would go out in my family’s house (which happened quite a few times) I’d make up boardgames or modify the ones we had so my siblings and I wouldn’t be bored.

Coding is my life, but when I do have free time I do like to unwind with some videogames. Occasionally, I'll also visit some museums because I love learning about other people's lives and seeing their work. My favorite one I've been to so far was the International Spy Museum in Washington D.C.

I am now looking for a software development position in the New York area and I am also open to relocating.
